MODGUIDE

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information ModGuide collects, why, and how it's handled. ModGuide does not sell your data, and does not run ads.

1. Information We Collect

WhatWhy
Email address, passwordAccount creation and login (handled securely via Supabase Auth — we never see or store your raw password)
Vehicles you add to your garage (make, model, year, trim, mileage, mods, service history)To power your maintenance tracker and build recommendations — this is the core function of the App
Name, email, vehicle, location, and job details submitted through On-SiteTo evaluate and respond to service requests. This is emailed directly to the operator and is not stored in a separate marketing database.

2. Third-Party Services We Use

ModGuide relies on a small number of infrastructure providers to operate:

  • Supabase — handles account authentication and stores your garage data
  • Resend — delivers On-Site booking notification emails
  • Vercel — hosts the App

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing infrastructure-level data (like IP addresses in server logs). We only share what's necessary for the App to function — we don't sell or rent your data to anyone.

3. What We Don't Do

  • We don't display ads or sell your data to advertisers
  • We don't share your garage data with other users unless you explicitly choose to (e.g. a future shareable build page)
  • We don't use your On-Site request details for anything beyond fulfilling that request

4. Your Choices

You can update or delete the vehicles in your garage at any time from within the App. To request full account deletion or a copy of your stored data, email us — we'll handle it directly.

5. Data Retention

Your account and garage data are retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, associated garage data is removed from our active database. On-Site request emails may persist in the operator's inbox as a normal record of business communication.

6. Children's Privacy

ModGuide is not directed at children. You must be at least 18 years old to create an account.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the date above. Significant changes affecting how your data is used will be communicated directly where possible.

8. Contact

Questions about your data or this Policy? Reach out at mattjustice1999@gmail.com.
